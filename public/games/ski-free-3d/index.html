SKIFREE 3D

A retro-modern alpine survival adventure.

How to Ski

Move Mouse Steer Left / Right (reversed steering)
Left-Click Jump over obstacles & perform tricks
Mobile Touch Drag to steer / Tap screen to jump
Speed Builds up automatically (Turn sharply to brake!)

The Mission

Ski down the infinite slope! Avoid trees, rocks, and gates. Catch big air off ramps for style points. Watch out for the **Abominable Snowman** who chases you after 2,000 meters!
